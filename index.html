Frequently asked questions

How long does the entire process take?

The complete process typically takes 6-8 weeks. Interview sessions last 1-2 hours each, spaced over 1-2 weeks based on your energy and schedule. Your comfort remains our priority throughout.

Should I prepare anything before we begin?

No formal preparation needed! Our interviewers will guide you through meaningful conversations. Gathering some cherished photographs beforehand can enrich your story and help rediscover forgotten memories.

What if I don't feel comfortable being recorded?

Most clients initially feel nervous but quickly forget the camera is there. Our interviewers create natural conversations that feel like chatting with a friend. For those who prefer alternatives, we offer audio recordings with photographs or written memoirs instead of video.
